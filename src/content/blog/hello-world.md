Welcome to my blog! This is my first post, written in Obsidian and published via the vault-to-Astro pipeline.

## How This Works

1. I write posts in my Obsidian vault under `5 Blog/posts/`
2. A GitHub Action syncs them to the Astro blog's content collection
3. Astro builds the static site with the new content

This setup gives me the best of both worlds: Obsidian's excellent writing experience and Astro's fast, modern static site generation.

## What to Expect

I'll be writing about software engineering, tools I find useful, and things I learn along the way. Stay tuned!
